French Army Innovation Challenge
Programming a drone swarm with distributed intelligence for rescue missions
French Army Innovation Lab, ISAE-SUPAERO, Ecole Polytechnique, ENSTA
Disclaimer: Due to confidentiality reasons I cannot disclose details about this challenge.

The main goal of this challenge was to develop an algorithm that would make 5 drones work together on a mission. In our case the mission was to spot people that are hurt in an indoor cluttered environment.

Technologies used: Python, ROS, Gazebo, QT, Git, Matlab, Simulink
